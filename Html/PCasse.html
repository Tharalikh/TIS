Lab: Assemble PC

Overview

We also had a lab exercise which is PC assembling. First of all, they asked us to disassemble the PC then assemble it back.

Reflection

It was a fun experience where we learned to disassemble a PC by:

  • Open the case by unscrew it
  • Take off the cable
  • Take off the Power supply
  • Take off the RAM
  • Take off the Hardisk Drive
  • Take off the fan

And then we assemble it back by doing it backward together with my group.
